Find books whose name match all terms you provide

  1. a term is a sequence of non-space characters; 'PDF', '65jw' and 'golang' are terms.
  2. term-matching is always case-insensitive.
  3. an ALL-CAPS term matches only whole words in book names
  4. a non-ALL-CAPS term matches a book name containing the same term text.
  5. an ebook-file's extension (e.g. pdf, epub) is part of its name and thus a word search candidate.
  6. EX: 'bo PDF' matches 'book.pdf' but not 'bopdfok.epub'. 'my p' matches 'my.book.pdf' and 'myopia.epub'
