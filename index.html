File-Collection-Searcher Find books whose name match all terms you provide

  1. a term is a sequence of non-space characters; 'PDF', '65jw', 'C++' and 'golang' are terms.
  2. term-matching is always case-insensitive.
  3. an ALL-CAPS term matches only whole words in book names.
  4. a non-ALL-CAPS term matches a book name containing the same term text.
  5. an ebook-file's extension (e.g. pdf, epub) is part of its name and thus a word search candidate.
  6. Book filename notes and search suggestions:
    • Fiction book names seldom contain copyright year, and title may be truncated (author name is typically most reliable).
    • Non-fiction book names generally DO NOT contain author name.
    • [Non-fiction] Edition info ("2e", "3e", etc.) is optional.
    • It's rarely useful to search for copyright year or edition as these are optional and even if present are not guaranteed to be correct.
    • DON'T GIVE UP -- keep trying: start general and make search more specific to eliminate uninteresting items.
  7. EX: 'bo PDF' matches 'book.pdf' but not 'bopdfok.epub'. 'my p' matches 'my.book.pdf' and 'myopia.epub'
